Digital Data Layer Validator

Type in an url to check whether it conforms to the Digital Data Layer standard. The Digital Data Layer (DDL) is a standardised way of exposing data on your eCommerce site. If you implement to the specification, it will mean that you will not have to duplicate your work to expose data to multiple providers (e.g. Triggered Messaging, an analytics provider and a tag management provider).
